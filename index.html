Metal Bellows

We design and manufacture standard metal bellow elements that can be assembled into customized expansion joint assemblies according to your requirements. Our metal bellows are available in single ply , double ply , or multi-ply (upto seven) configurations and come in various material options.

Read More
Universal expansion joints

We design and manufacture a wide range of Expansion Joints designs, from single bellows element to pressure balanced options, for your piping or ducting requirements. Our Expansion Joints are used in various industries, including Oil and Gas , Aerospace , Power Generation , Petrochemical , and Water and Wastewater .

Dismentling Joints

We specialize in the manufacturing of Exhaust Bellows for a range of engines, including: Caterpillar Engines, Waukesha Engines, Solar Gas,Turbines,White Superior Engines, ALCO Engines. We take pride in delivering superior-quality Exhaust Bellows that effectively reduce vibration and provide the necessary flexibility for optimal engine performance.
